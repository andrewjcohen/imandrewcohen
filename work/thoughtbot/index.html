thoughtbot

Pilsner Cohen Party Next Big Sound

Working alongside my talented co-workers I helped conceptualize, design, and develop the latest iteration of thoughtbot.com. Corey Haggard set the initial design direction while I helped with UX and Interaction Design. Our aim was to create a more modern and simplified portfolio site to showcase the quality work we do here at thoughtbot. On the front-end the site was built using Bourbon, Neat, Bitters, and SASS.

Just like all great designs, the job is never done. Tons of iterations are always in the pipeline, but for now check out the live site at thoughtbot.com.
